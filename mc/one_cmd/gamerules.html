Gamerules in One Command

About:

This is a simple one command that allows you to change simple minecraft gamerules such as keep inventory, mob griefing, and more! It allows other players to see health of other players online. Also, it creates a death scoreboard (shows player deaths) and 2 teams so you may add different players to different teams! (Team1=blue color name) (Team2-green color name).
