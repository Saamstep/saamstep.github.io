Import a Minecraft World

  • Windows
  • Mac

1. Go to %appdata%\.minecraft
2. Open the Saves folder
3. Drag and drop the world (in UNZIPPED form) into the folder.
4. Open Minecraft (in 1.9 or above)
5. Go to Singleplayer
6. Play selected world

1. Open Finder 
2. Click Go to Folder and type in ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft. 
3. Open the Saves folder 
4. Drag and drop the world (in UNZIPPED form) into the folder. 
5. Open Minecraft (in 1.9 or above) 
6. Go to Singleplayer. 
7. Play selected world
